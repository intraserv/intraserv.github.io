WELCOME

“you come to love, not by finding the perfect person, but by learning to see an imperfect person perfectly”

Welcome to the perfectly imperfect world of Nicci and Ryan. It’s not long now until these worlds are joined in Holy Madtrimony!!!! We are delighted, excited, scared stiff of what’s about to take place and we would be honoured if you could join us and provide some much needed moral support. We were brought together by the World wide web, nothing as romantic as a drunken 2am snog in a spit and sawdust club, nor an equally romantic office romance. We were unaware that each other existed despite the fact we may well have slept in the same tent at Cuffley camp back in the early 80’s, or been sat across the table from each other at Bushey sports club whilst tucking into a perfectly average sunday roast. Ironically our first date was a Sunday lunch at a mutually agreeable pub in Bearsted, we spent most of the date critiquing a table of four and their choice of knitted jumpers. I think this was a blessed relief for both of us as first dates tend to be awkward, nervous occasions. Two years have passed, flown by in fact (not a knitted jumper in sight).

And here we are, arranging our wedding. Together and with the love and support of our boys and our family and friends we realised that we have experienced most of the things life can throw at us and that it was time we made things official. I met Nicci at a time in my life when i couldn’t imagine what my future held, from the moment i saw her get out of her car in the carpark of that pub I knew my life was about to change. But i didn’t realise just how much. I’m not a religious man, but i really have been blessed. She appears to see an imperfect person perfectly (should’ve gone to Specsavers) So please dust off your glad rags, make sure you’ve eaten, because we intend to kick off the rest of our lives in style with the assistance of an alcoholic drink or two and would love to do so in the company of our friends and families. See you there xxx
